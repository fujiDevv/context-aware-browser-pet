Did You Know? 🐾

Interesting tidbits about your desktop companion:

  • Clawd's mood fluctuates based on the websites you visit! Tech blogs keep him focused, while social media makes him playful.
  • At level 50, Clawd can undergo Prestige Rebirth, unlocking faster baseline speeds and new cosmetics.
  • Leaving your cursor idle for more than 45 seconds triggers Clawd to wander, skateboard, or read a tiny book!
  • Clawd gets cozy in the winter holidays and spooky in October with seasonal costumes automatically.
